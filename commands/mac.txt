To print open ports
	$ sudo lsof -iTCP -sTCP:LISTEN -n -P

To print detail of specific port
	$ sudo lsof -iTCP:80 -sTCP:LISTEN -n -P
	$ sudo lsof -nP -iTCP:80 -sTCP:LISTEN
